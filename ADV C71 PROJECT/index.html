Campus Life

The St.Therese of Lisieux auditorium

The St.Therese of Lisieux auditorium is definitely a pride colossal structure of the school which is equipped with modern audio visual facilities and has a massive seating capacity of approximately 800. The sound proof auditorium is fitted with a projector, modern lighting systems including flood light and spot lights, good acoustic system and is fully air-conditioned. It hosts numerous events including annual day award functions, Inter School Competitions, Investiture ceremony, Alumni celebrations. The morning assembly and various competitions which are held in this auditorium give a variety of exposure to the students in showcasing their talents and groom their inborn virtues.

Computer Lab

The Computer Lab in our school is equipped with computing facility with Dual Core computers connected through the Local Area Network. This lab features 65 personal-computing workstations with Microsoft Windows 7 and Office 2007 application, with printer and scanner facility. Microsoft Windows software is available for word processing, statistics, spreadsheets and database management. Students use computers in their day-to-day study in order to meet the challenges of modern learning and to keep pace with global scenario. The lab is also equipped with a new 3D Printing Technology and has a latest 3D printer that allows students to print 3D objects.

Basketball Court

For many students, playing basketball at school is the highlight of the day. The School houses a basketball court with dimensions74 feet long and 42 feet wide. The playing surface produces true bounce and excellent grip in all weather conditions.
